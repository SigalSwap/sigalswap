# SigalSwap Privacy Policy

**Last Modified: [DATE]**

SigalSwap LLC (the "Company", "we", "us", or "our") operates the SigalSwap
web interface (the "Interface"). This Privacy Policy describes what
information we collect, what we don't collect, and how we handle your data.

We built SigalSwap for people who value privacy. This policy reflects that.

---

## 1. What We Do NOT Collect

- **No analytics or tracking.** We do not use Google Analytics, Mixpanel,
  Hotjar, or any other analytics service.
- **No cookies.** The Interface does not set cookies of any kind — no session
  cookies, no tracking cookies, no third-party cookies.
- **No wallet tracking.** We do not log, store, or associate your wallet
  address with any identifying information.
- **No IP logging.** We do not log your IP address on our application servers.
- **No fingerprinting.** We do not use browser fingerprinting, device
  fingerprinting, or any other tracking technique.
- **No advertising.** We do not serve ads or share data with advertisers.
- **No social media trackers.** The Interface does not embed social media
  widgets, pixels, or tracking scripts.

## 2. What We May Collect

### 2.1 Infrastructure Logs
Our hosting provider may automatically collect standard web server logs
(IP addresses, request timestamps, HTTP headers) as part of normal server
operation. These logs are used solely for security monitoring, abuse
prevention, and debugging. They are not correlated with wallet addresses
or user identities, and are deleted on a rolling basis.

### 2.2 Blockchain Data
When you submit a transaction through the Interface, that transaction is
processed by the Aztec Network. On-chain data (including transaction details
that are publicly visible) is recorded on the blockchain permanently. This is
inherent to blockchain technology and is not controlled by the Company. The
Aztec Network's privacy features may shield certain transaction details, but
the Company makes no guarantee about the extent of on-chain privacy (see
Section 4).

### 2.3 Voluntary Communications
If you contact us via email or other channels, we retain the content of those
communications to respond to you. We do not share this information with third
parties.

## 3. Third-Party Services

The Interface may interact with third-party services that have their own
privacy practices, including:

- **Wallet providers** (e.g., browser extensions, hardware wallets) — we do
  not control what data your wallet collects
- **RPC/node providers** — if the Interface connects to third-party Aztec
  nodes, those providers may log connection metadata
- **Hosting providers** — our web hosting infrastructure may collect standard
  server logs as described in Section 2.1

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services
you use in connection with the Interface.

## 4. Privacy on Aztec

The Aztec Network provides privacy features through zero-knowledge
cryptography. These features are a property of the underlying blockchain, not
something added by the Company. The Company does not control, enhance, or
diminish the privacy guarantees of the Aztec Network.

Privacy on Aztec is not absolute. Certain information (such as pool reserves
and trade amounts) is publicly visible by design. User identity may be
protected by zero-knowledge proofs, but this protection depends on the
correct functioning of cryptographic systems, the Aztec protocol, and user
behavior. See the Terms of Service (Section 7) for a full discussion of
privacy limitations.

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or
governmental request, or if we believe in good faith that disclosure is
necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of the Company, its
users, or the public.

## 6. Data Retention

- **Infrastructure logs:** Deleted on a rolling basis (typically 14-30 days,
  depending on hosting provider defaults).
- **Voluntary communications:** Retained as long as necessary to respond and
  for reasonable record-keeping.
- **Blockchain data:** Permanent and immutable. We cannot delete on-chain
  data.

## 7. Children

The Interface is not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not
knowingly collect information from children.

## 8. International Users

The Interface is operated from the United States. If you access the Interface
from another jurisdiction, you acknowledge that your information may be
processed in the United States. By using the Interface, you consent to such
processing.

## 9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your personal
data, including the right to access, correct, or delete information we hold
about you. Given that we collect minimal data (Section 1), these rights are
largely moot. If you have questions or requests, contact us at contact@sigalswap.com.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be
indicated by updating the "Last Modified" date. Your continued use of the
Interface constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
